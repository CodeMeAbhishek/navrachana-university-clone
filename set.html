SCHOOL OF

ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

In a fast-evolving world, Engineering as a subject becomes critical as it is at the forefront of the change.

The School of Engineering and Technology (SET) with its holistic approach, industry-experienced faculty and state-of-the-art labs, equips students to lead that change.

International and industry collaborations, as well as the importance given to compulsory internships and project-based learning, help the students match pace with the constantly changing narratives and create problem-solving leaders.
